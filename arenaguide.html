Arena Guide:
Take a look at the left-middle of the screen. You will currently see which zone you are at by looking over now. There are 4 kinds of zones: Safe Zone, Normal Zone, Combat Zone, and lastly Free PK Zone.

I'll explain each, one at a time. First of all, Safe Zones are safe, meaning that adventurers cannot attack each other within them. Only fights against monsters are allowed. The whole area within this place is safe for inexperienced adventurers, but keep in mind that only certain areas such as towns are safe.

Next is Normal Zone. Most of the land is a normal zone. Besides monsters, even adventurers can attack each other and take lives. Such actions will invite the wrath of guards and build the killer's reputation. Fallen adventurers run the risk of losing experience and items.

Next is Combat Zone. These are narrow passageways, like a front lawn of a house. There are no negative effects against any party or players who attack each other within a Combat Zone. You will not be chased by the guards for attacking and there is no loss of experience points or items for those fallen.

Finally, there is Free PK Zone. This is a place for competition. Winners will receive no penalty or risk, just like in Combat Zones, but losers run the risk of losing experience.

This place is safe, but if you travel, you will find few others like it. Keep this in mind and be careful to defend yourself.
