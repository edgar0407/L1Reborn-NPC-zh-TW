Item: Enchant

Weapon Enchant:
Weapon Enchant is available by using Weapon Magic Scroll, Kalbas's Scroll and the Ancients' Alchemy Scroll.
There will be +1 Enchant with each successful Enchant, but the tried item will vaporize away if the Enchant fails.
Generally, weapons can be safely Enchanted up to +6, but for Bone, Black Mithril, there's a probability to fail from +1 Enchant attempt.

Armor Enchant:
Weapon Enchant is available by using Armor Magic Scroll, Gian's Scroll and the Ancients' Magic Scroll.
There will be +1 Enchant with each successful Enchant, but the tried item will vaporize away if the Enchant fails.
Generally, armor can be safely Enchanted up to +4, but for Bone, Black Mithril, there's a probability to fail from +1 Enchant attempt.

Accessory Enchant:
Undecided

Blessing/Cursing Enchant:
With a successful Enchant with Blessed Scroll, 1~3 Enchant at a time is available.
With a successful Enchant with Cursed Scroll, -1 Enchant is possible.

When Enchant is not allowed:
If not Weapon/Armor/Accessory, or when items are Sealed no Enchant is available.
Also when Enchant is attempted on +9 and above items, if the result is invalid (besides successful/failed), there will be no Enchant. For some items, No Enchant Available is programmed.
