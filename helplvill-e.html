Level Up classified by Class: Illusionist
Beginning of Illusionist

The name of the place where Illusionist starts is a town called 'Hidden Valley'.

'Hidden Valley' is a town for beginners which has many convenient facilities for beginners. Up to level 12 may stay here and above level 12 will be automatically expelled from 'Hidden Valley'.

In the middle of 'Hidden Valley' town, there is an NPC who casts 'Haste' magic for free which increases movement speed, and healers who recover HP are available throughout the town .

Find 'Scarecrow' outside of 'Hidden Valley' for training to safely increase level up to 5.

Level 5

Level 5 allows you to use the 'Warehouse'. The storage House is located in each town. It does not cost anything to place items, but it costs a little to find them.

Level 10

And you can learn Level 1 Illusion Magic of Illusionist.

Level 11

Starting level 11, Experience Points will decrease when the character dies so hunting requires a caution.

Level 15

Level 15 allows you to execute the first Wizard Quest. The Quest can be executed when 'Elder Sleyin' of Silveria is visited.

Level 20

And you can learn Level 2 Illusion Magic of Illusionist.

Level 30

Level 30 allows 'Full Chatting' to communicate with all users within the current server. Place [ & ] before your message.

Level 30 allows you to execute the second Wizard Quest. The Quest can be executed when 'Elder Sleyin' of Silveria is visited.

And you can learn Level 3 Illusion Magic of Illusionist.

Level 40

Level 40 allows you to create a 'Title' for your own character.

The command is
[ /Title 'yourID' 'desired title' ] .

And you can learn Level 4 Illusion Magic of Illusionist.

Level 45

Level 45 allows you to execute the third Wizard Quest. The Quest can be executed when 'Elder Sleyin' of Silveria is visited.

Level 50

Level 50 allows you to execute the fourth Wizard Quest. The Quest can be executed when 'Elder Sleyin' of Silveria is visited.

Level 51

All characters will be given +1 bonus point on their own status from level 51. Invest the bonus point on the status you would like to raise.
