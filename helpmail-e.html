Sending Mail:

Click , enter the recipient, title, and content, then click the send button to send the mail. In order to send mail, you need 50 Adena for ordinary mail and 1000 adena for pledge mail. When the recipient's mailbox becomes full, you cannot send any more mail.

Mailbox Explanation:
You can save up to 20 ordinary mail and 50 pledge mail.
You cannot receive any more mail once your mailbox becomes full. You can see the mail's content by double-clicking on the mail on the list.
The pledge mail is a bulletin board type. All pledge members can see it at the same time.
Please use the reply icon to send a reply.
The delete icon is used to delete the mail. Pledge mail can only be deleted by the sender or the pledge lord.
The save icon is used to save mail.
The list icon is for moving it to the list window.


Save Explanation:
You can save up to 10 mail. Just like your mailbox, you can see the mail's content by double-clicking on the list. You can send mail that you wish to keep for a long time to the Save Box by using the save icon.
