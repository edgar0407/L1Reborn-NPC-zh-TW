Game System: Alliance
General Information on Alliance:
Maximum 4 clans can make an alliance with each other. Even if they are in alliance, each clan manages the Clan System except a castle siege.

Joining Alliance and withdrawal:
Forming an alliance is available to level 25 and above and for the lords who owns clans (except alliance lords).
The lord character directly faces the other lord who he/she wishes to make an alliance with, and use /Join Alliance command to form an alliance. If the other person agrees, the two clans become allied.
/Alliance command can bring up the name of each allied clan, number of total members, and number of currently connected people. /Alliance Withdrawal command can cancel an alliance.

Alliance Chatting:
If you place ~ in front of Chatting Contents, you can use Alliance Chatting.
