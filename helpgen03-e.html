Menu Icon Bar Use:
There are 7 Menu Icon Bars on the rightside bottom of the screen. Clicking on each icon will allow the following functions.

Help Window: Click or use
Ctrl+H.

Status Window: Click or use
Ctrl+A key.

Magic Window: Click or use
Ctrl+S key.

Item Window: Click or use
Tab key.

Community Window: Click or use
Ctrl+C key.

Option Window: Click or use
Ctrl+V key.

Game Termination Window: Click or use
Ctrl+Q or Alt+X key.
